ABOUT

DT Saw Sharpening is open to consultants from all practices with any skillset (developers, project managers, business analysts, testers, cloud engineers, et al)the only requirement is the desire to learn!

 

Our group offers opportunities for applied learning, professional development, mentoring and community in an engaging and relaxed environment. Our aim is to facilitate unit growth in new technologies, best-practices and the chance to put this knowledge into practice.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

TEAM

The DT Columbus Saw Sharpening offers hands-on exposure to emerging and leading-edge technologies using a collaborative development approach. The group leverages shared development environments to facilitate pair programming and Extreme Programming exercises and projects!
